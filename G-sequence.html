Mathematical Problem 1: Geometric Sequence
Formula: an = a1r^n-1
• Ted was studying bacteria on the first day of school of the week, and noticed that there was a pattern in their behavior. On the first day of observation, the count of the bacteria was 6. By the second day, it had grown to 24. On the third day, the bacteria had grown to 96. Ted wants to study the growth of the bacteria and understand its patterns for a week. What would be the count of the bacteria next Monday? 		Given:
a1 = 6
r = 24 ÷ 6 = 4
n = 8

Solution:
a8 = 6·4^8-1
a8 = 6·4^7
a8 = 6·16,384
a8 = 98,304
 • The bacteria would have grown to 98,304 by next Monday.
Mathematical Problem 2: Geometric Series
Formula: Sn = a1(1-r^n) / (1-r)
• A lawyer's salary doubles every year. If her salary in 2020 is Php100,000, how much would her salary be in 2025? 		Given:
a1 = 100,000
n = 6
r = 2

Solution:
100,000 (1 - 2^6) / (1 - 2)
100,000 (-63) / -1
-6,300,000 / -1
a6 = 6,300,000
 • The lawyer's salary would be Php6,300,000 by 2025.
Mathematical Problem 3: Geometric Mean
Formula: an = a1r^n-1
• In a span of a rapid 8 seconds, a post about a famous celebrity dying has gone viral on social media, starting with only 6 views on the first second and 468,750 views on the 8th. How many views did the tragic social media post have 7 seconds after it was posted, calculating using the geometric mean? 		Given:
a1 = 6
a8 = 468,750
n = 8

Solution:
an = a1r^n-1
468,750 = 6r^8-1
468,750 / 6 = r^7
r = 5

6, 30, 150, 750, 3750, 18750, 93750, 468750
 • The social media post had 93,750 views 7 seconds after it was posted.
