Mathematical Problem 1: Fibonacci Sequence (Binet's Formula)
Formula: ( (1 + √5) / 2 )^n/√5
• A student has an assignment that is due in one week. She starts writing for one hour on the first day and one hour on the second day. How many hours did she take to finish it one week later? Show the progression of the time she took to do it each day. 		Given:
Day 1: 1 hour
Day 2: 1 hour
Day 3: 2 hours (1+1)
Day 4: 3 hours (1+2)
Day 5: 5 hours (2+3)
Day 6: 8 hours (3+5)
Day 7: 13 hours (5+13)

Solution:
( (1 + √5) / 2 )^7/√5
= 12.98
= 13
 • By day 7, she wrote for 13 hours.
Mathematical Problem 2:
• An artist finishes 2 drawings in one day, on the second day she finishes 2 drawings. How many drawings will the artist have finished in 7 days? Show the progression of finished drawings for each day. 		Solution:
Day 1: 2
Day 2: 2
Day 3: 4 (2+2)
Day 4: 6 (4+2)
Day 5: 10 (6+4)
Day 6: 16 (6+10)
Day 7: 26 (10+16)
 • By day 7, the artist will have 26 finished drawings.
Mathematical Problem 3:
• An online seller started small with 16 buyers in her first week. She noticed that her buyer count was stagnant the second week, but noticed a growth by the third. How many buyers would she end up having by the eight week? Show the progression of buyers each week. 		Solution:
Week 1: 16
Week 2: 16
Week 3: 32 (16+16)
Week 4: 48 (16+32)
Week 5: 80 (32+48)
Week 6: 128 (48+80)
Week 7: 208 (80+128)
Week 8: 336 (128+208)
 • By week 8, the online seller would have 336 buyers.
